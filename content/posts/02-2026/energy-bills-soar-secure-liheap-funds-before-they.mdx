## Start here: what to do first

Applying for LIHEAP starts with understanding your eligibility and gathering necessary documents. Here's a straightforward roadmap to get you started:

1. **Check Eligibility**: Eligibility is generally based on income, household size, and energy costs. Be sure to verify specific state requirements.
2. **Gather Documentation**: You'll need proof of income, utility bills, and possibly other documents like identification or lease agreements.
3. **Submit Application Early**: LIHEAP funds are limited. Applying early in the heating season increases your chances of receiving help.

## FAQs

### How can I apply for LIHEAP?
You can apply for LIHEAP through your local agency or state website. Applications are often available online or in-person.

### What documents do I need for LIHEAP?
Typically, you need identification, proof of income, and recent utility bills. Check with your local office for any additional requirements.

### When should I apply for LIHEAP?
Apply as early as possible in the heating season, as funds are limited and can run out quickly.

### What if my application is denied?
You can appeal a denial decision. Contact your local LIHEAP office for guidance on the appeals process.
