## FAQs

### What are the common eligibility requirements for rent relief?
Typically, you must demonstrate financial hardship, have an income below a certain threshold, and be at risk of housing instability.

### How long does it take to receive rent relief funds?
The timeline can vary, but many states aim to process applications within a few weeks.

### Can I apply for rent relief if I have already received assistance before?
In some cases, yes. Check with your local program to see if repeat applications are permitted.

### What should I do if my application is denied?
You can appeal the decision or seek further assistance from local housing authorities or legal aid organizations.
