## FAQs

### What is the Child Tax Credit?
The Child Tax Credit is a tax benefit designed to help families with the cost of raising children. It provides a credit per qualifying child.

### How might the Child Tax Credit change next year?
Congress is considering proposals that could increase the credit amount, expand eligibility, and offer monthly payments.

### Who qualifies for the Child Tax Credit?
Eligibility is typically based on income, filing status, and the age of the children in the household.

### How do income phase-outs affect the Child Tax Credit?
Income phase-outs reduce the credit amount as household income increases, potentially eliminating the credit for higher-income families.
