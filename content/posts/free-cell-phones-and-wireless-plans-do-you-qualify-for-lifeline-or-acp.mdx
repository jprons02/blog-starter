## Introduction

The Lifeline program and the Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP) are federal initiatives designed to provide affordable communication services, including free cell phones and wireless plans, to low-income individuals. These programs aim to bridge the digital divide and ensure that everyone has access to essential communication tools.

## Understanding Lifeline and ACP

### What is the Lifeline Program?

The Lifeline program is a government assistance program that offers a monthly subsidy to help eligible low-income consumers access voice and broadband services. It provides a discount on qualifying telephone or broadband service for eligible subscribers, making it easier for them to stay connected.

### What is the Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP)?

The Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP) is a new program that aims to help low-income households afford internet service or connected devices. It provides a discount of up to $30 per month for broadband service and up to a $75 discount on a connected device.

## Qualifying for Assistance

To qualify for the Lifeline program and the Affordable Connectivity Program, individuals must meet certain eligibility criteria based on income, participation in federal assistance programs, or the Tribal government. It's essential to understand the eligibility requirements and the application process to determine if you qualify for these programs.

If you're also trying to lower internet costs, see [ACP Internet Discounts](./how-to-get-free-or-discounted-internet-through-federal-programs).

## Applying for Lifeline or ACP

Applying for the Lifeline program or the Affordable Connectivity Program involves a simple application process. Individuals can apply through participating providers or directly with the Universal Service Administrative Company (USAC). The USAC oversees both programs and ensures that eligible consumers can benefit from the assistance.

- A practical tip: When applying for Lifeline or ACP, be sure to have your proof of eligibility documentation ready to expedite the application process.

1. Benefits of Lifeline and ACP

Both the Lifeline program and the Affordable Connectivity Program offer significant benefits to eligible individuals, including access to free or discounted cell phones, wireless plans, and broadband services. By participating in these programs, individuals can stay connected with family, access essential resources, and improve their overall quality of life.
