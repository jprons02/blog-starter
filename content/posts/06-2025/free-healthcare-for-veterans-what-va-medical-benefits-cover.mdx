## FAQs

### Do I have to pay if I have Medicare or private insurance?
Often no for care tied to service-connected conditions; otherwise, copays can apply based on your priority group. VA may bill your private plan for non-service-connected care.

### Can my family use my VA health care?
Generally no. Spouses/dependents may qualify for CHAMPVA or TRICARE depending on your disability rating and status.

### Can I use urgent care outside VA?
If enrolled and eligible, you can use in-network community urgent care for minor issues; keep your VA ID handy and follow program rules about visit limits and notifications.
